## _Thorn's Revelation_

- [[Tirri-Kastner]] goes off to find [[Wanderer Gayland Thorn aka Vaelisse]]
	- they greet each other
	- TK asks about the vine growing on him
	- Thorn:  "it can be unsettling and a bit grotesque"
	- Thorn tells TK she's not a child any more, and must have a conversation that's long overdue... let me tell you a a story
	- tells TK the story of the three sisters of simeron
		- in the first world what you call the fey lands lived 3 fairy princesses,
		- they lived a carefree life of song and games, and were famous all across the realm for their beauty and grace.
		- they had many suiters, and the three competed for the most impressive desires by the suiters
		- the sister's need grew, and in time the people's love turned to jealousy, and fear
		- the fairy courts dealt with the three as they always do with powerful women.  They gathered their armies and drove them away and gave them a horrible name ... they named them "Hags"
		- Thorn says that Allora Castner is also a hag ... just ... like ... [[Tirri-Kastner]]
		- Thorn reveals her true self.  Fair skin, sharp eyes, purple dress that features mirrors
		- Thorn reveals that when TK was born, Thorn tested her.  She set up the encounter with the slavers in the woods where she ran away in fear for her life.  Thorn claims this was a strength and not a weakness because her morality didn't get in the way of greater plans.  She preserved her life which was worth more than those silly people who didn't matter.
		- Thorn compliments TK on putting _that boy_ to sleep and stealing the magic of his dreams to fuel the ritual.
		- Thorn monologs and asks TK to join her
		- Thorn gives TK her true name:  "Valise" (need to confirm spelling)
		- ![[Vaelisse.png]]
		- Thorn says that the vines on Rhen will continue to grow and it will continue to grant her power
		- TK asks if she has any friends.  "I don't have friends, i have sisters!" -Thorn
		- TK says she's begun to think of our party members as family.  Thorn says she already has sisters, a coven even!  And you don't have to pretend to care about your children anymore!
		- Thorn says she loves TK and that TK is her daughter
		- [[Wanderer Gayland Thorn aka Vaelisse]] says she is bigger than anything else we could be doing right now
		- Thorn says she is the only one who will love her, taunts her to tell her friends what she is, questions out loud what might happen if the wilted rose discovers her secret
		- Thorn tempts TK to come wander with her for a year and a day and she will reveal all (after TK has spent that time with her)
		- Thorn tells her to go sleep on what she's learned and give her an answer tomorrow
