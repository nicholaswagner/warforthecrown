Located in: [[Rosanna]]

The chamber where Rosanna's Imperial Senate convenes to vote on matters of state, including primogeniture. Features benches for senators, a raised dais, and a second-floor overlook. Also referred to as the **Imperial Senate**.

## Senate Floor Grounds

### Voritas Safe Room

A hidden Voritas safe room located beneath the Senate floor. Contains century-old murals, a mysterious glass orb, and was used as a refuge by the Voritas family.

### Arcade of Triumphs

A grand hall in Rosanna featuring high ceilings and artifacts on display. Banners of the Empire line the walls, featuring the Lion of Stavian and the Peacock of the Empress.
