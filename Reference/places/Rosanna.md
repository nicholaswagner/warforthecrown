## Locations of interest

### Abadar's Pillar

Abadar's Pillar — an enormous tower and the Grand Headquarters of the Glorian military. The balcony overlooks the site where [[Vice Count Rene Malbec du Valle]]'s public execution took place, witnessed by his son [[Caritas Zespire]] and others including [[General High Stratagos Maxillar Pythareus]].

### Crownsgate

An industrial district with a crumbling filthy wooden footbridge. Industrial buildings and warehouses — anything of value was stripped from here ages ago, even the cobblestones were stolen for other parts of the city.

#### Warehouse

A guarded warehouse in Crownsgate where [[Martella-Lotheed]] was being held captive. Six guards were posted, and the party infiltrated through a side door. Inside: piles of crates, a metal office overlook, and narrow pathways through 40ft high ceilings.
