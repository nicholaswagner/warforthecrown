Located in: [[Rosanna]]

Also known as the Repository of the Dignified. A vast, otherworldly library and archive maintained by Viscountess Octavia Nicodemus. Contains hidden passages, a preserved garden room, a wax gallery, and a reading room accessed via a mysterious circle door trap with a whiskey smell, past a preserved garden room with garden oozes.

---
## Points of Interest

### Museum of Conquests

A large chamber within the Repository of the Dignified, holding display cases filled with suits of armor, banners, and standards. Contains the "Museum of Conquests" with a security alarm system that activates animated armor.

### Wax Gallery

A gallery within the Repository of the Dignified featuring dozens of silhouettes of people in ballgowns and finery (shadows of them) and an ooze-wig.

### Theater

A theater within the Repository of the Dignified where King Dodgio appeared and attacked the party with curving bullets.

### Underground Passages

The underground passages accessed from the Repository of the Dignified. Contains artifacts, tripwire javelins, storage rooms, and leads to the Spider Lair.

#### Spider Lair

A spider lair in the underground passages. Features spider eyes in the ceiling, a dining room with a puppet-haunted dead halfling girl, and a partially-covered steel shield stuck in fungus.
