# Stachys

A small, impoverished barony in Meratt County. So obscure it does not appear 
on most maps. Fell into disrepair after its previous nobleman died and was 
left without stewardship. The party was appointed as its Tribunes by Princess 
Eutropia following the Exaltation Day Massacre.

**Base of operations:** [[Swanmere]] manor
**County:** Meratt (under [[Count Bartleby Lotheed]])
**Town Level** `3`

---

## Town Notes
- Poor and underdeveloped but showing signs of recovery under new leadership
- Town loyalty increased after the party defeated the manticore
- Market recently opened; improvements ongoing (125gp contributed by Moost)
- Full moon brings the reconstitution of [[Marjorine Sandaris]]
