## Palace Grounds

The Palace of Birdsong encompasses a sprawling estate with formal gardens, recreational grounds, and private wings. The following are key areas within its grounds:

### Truco Field

An open field on the palace grounds used for the truco card game. Where Rhenquist played and won a truco match against Titus Casava-Lotheed, and where the Archbanker gossiped with Tirri about the succession question, during the Tanager Jubilee.

### Palace Library

A library within the Palace of Birdsong. Where the "Scooby Gang" (Rhenquist, Arturo, Tirri) hunted for it, finding a reflecting pool with angel statues and pure white marble, and where Arturo saw a mysterious black circle tattoo in his reflection.

### Garden Courtyard

A courtyard featuring flowers, shrubberies, and multiple greenhouses. Where Princess Eutropia Stavian gave her primogeniture speech and where a female servant with a purple brick was spotted before it was passed through a conspiracy, during the Exaltation.

### Jousting Grounds

An open area on the palace grounds used for the jousting tournament. Where Arturo and Caritas both fell from their horses during the Tanager Jubilee jousting match.
