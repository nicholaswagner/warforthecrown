A sprawling noble estate on the outskirts of Rosanna, host to the Tanager Jubilee and the Exaltation.

## Notes

- Where the PCs meet for the first time and spend their summer
- [[Princess Eutropia Stavian]] purchased this in secret using [[Lady Glorianna Morilla]] as a proxy at auction for an exorbitant price

---

## Palace Grounds

The Palace of Birdsong encompasses a sprawling estate with formal gardens, recreational grounds, and private wings. The following are key areas within its grounds:

### Truco Field

An open field on the palace grounds used for the truco card game. Where [[Rhenquist-Merosett|Rhenquist]] played and won a truco match against [[Titus Casava-Lotheed]], and where the [[Archbanker Lady Paril]] gossiped with [[Tirri-Kastner|Tirri]] about the succession question, during the [[Tanager Jubilee]].

### Palace Library

A library within the Palace of Birdsong. Where the "Scooby Gang" ([[Rhenquist-Merosett|Rhenquist]], [[Arturo Basri|Arturo]], [[Tirri-Kastner|Tirri]]) hunted for clues, finding a reflecting pool with angel statues and pure white marble, and where Arturo saw a mysterious black circle tattoo in his reflection, during the [[Tanager Jubilee]].

### Garden Courtyard

A courtyard featuring flowers, shrubberies, and multiple greenhouses. Where [[Princess Eutropia Stavian]] gave her primogeniture speech and where a female servant with a purple brick was spotted before it was passed through a conspiracy, during the [[Exaltation]].

### Jousting Grounds

An open area on the palace grounds used for the jousting tournament. Where [[Arturo Basri|Arturo]] and [[Caritas Zespire|Caritas]] both fell from their horses during the [[Tanager Jubilee]] jousting match.
